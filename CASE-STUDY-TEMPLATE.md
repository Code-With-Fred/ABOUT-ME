### Client: [Fashion Retailer Name]
**Location:** [Lagos / Port Harcourt / Abuja]
**Industry:** Fashion & Retail
**Challenge:** Client wanted to expand online but didn't have an e-commerce website.

### The Challenge
- Physical store only; no online sales channel
- Losing sales to competitors with online presence
- Manual order taking via phone/WhatsApp

### Our Solution
We built a custom e-commerce website including:

- **Inventory management system** that syncs real-time stock
- **Order management dashboard** for order tracking and fulfillment
- **Mobile-optimized design** for shopping on phones
- **SEO optimization** to rank for local keywords

### Results (After 6 Months)

- ✓ **₦2.5M in additional revenue** from online sales
- ✓ **40% of customers** from outside their primary location
- ✓ **Ranked #1** for "Fashion Store [City]"
- ✓ **30% reduction** in time spent on order management

### Technologies Used
- Frontend: React + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js + Express
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Hosting: Vercel + AWS
